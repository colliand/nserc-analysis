** NSERC Open Data Exploration
*** About

   Canada's [[http://open.canada.ca/en][Open Government Portal]] includes [[http://open.canada.ca/data/en/dataset/c1b0f627-8c29-427c-ab73-33968ad9176e][NSERC Awards Data]] from 1995 through 2016. The awards data (in .csv format) were copied to an [[http://docs.aws.amazon.com/AmazonS3/latest/dev/UsingBucket.html][Amazon Web Services S3 bucket]].

   This site explores the NSERC Awards Data with the goal of helping Canada to achieve the best outcomes for research and training of future researchers.
